Services

What Can I Do For You?

I am well-prepared in providing the following services for any potential employers or clients.

Business Analysis

Business Analysis is my most frequent practice before any product design, by defining needs and recommending roadmaps & solutions that deliver value to stakeholders.

Prototyping

Before designing a product, there will be a picture I create in my mind. Transferring this picture from my mind into a visible environment is prototyping. A prototype often contains simple elements like wireframes & color blocks, being used for verifying the core business flow and it’s much more concise than UI.

PRD Composition

A product requirements document (PRD) is a document containing all the requirements to a certain product. I always provide a PRD, along with the corresponding prototype, to the development team.

UI Design

User interface (UI) design is the process I use to build interfaces in software or games, focusing on actual looks or style of the product. Generally I am not into this work but I am able to handle it if I am required to do so.

Project Management

From time to time I will play the role of a project manager. I am used to agile development, and experienced of managing a team of 5-10 people. In most cases, I will use Jira, Confluence and Slack to manage and follow up the project.

Cross-cultural communication

My language proficiency in English, Chinese and Japanese, and understanding & respect to cultural differences helps me to orgnize people with various nationalities, styles of working, age, race, ethnicity, gender, etc... to effectively engage within a same project.
