Services

What Can I Do For You?

I am well-equipped to provide potential employers or clients with the following services:

Business Analysis

As a business analyst, I focus on identifying needs and recommending roadmaps and solutions that deliver value to stakeholders before any product design is initiated.

Prototyping

Translating mental images into a visible environment is what I describe as prototyping. The prototype usually contains simple elements like wireframes and color blocks that help in verifying the core business flow without including UI design.

PRD Composition

A product requirements document (PRD) is the definitive guide that captures all the requirements for a particular product. I always provide a comprehensive PRD, along with the corresponding prototype, to the DevOps team.

UI Design

User interface (UI) design is the process of building interfaces in software or games, which requires exceptional skills to design for a product’s desired style and look. Although my focus is on other areas of design and development, I can handle UI design when required.

Project Management

I am experienced in playing the role of a project manager, focusing on agile development and capable of managing a team of 5-10 people with tools like Jira, Confluence, and Slack.

Cross-cultural communication

My language proficiency in English, Chinese, and Japanese, coupled with my understanding and respect for cultural differences, has made me effective at managing and organizing people with various backgrounds, ages, races, ethnicities, genders, etc. to effectively engage within a project.
